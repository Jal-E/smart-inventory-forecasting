M5 Dataset: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)¶

In this notebook, we explore and prepare the official Walmart M5 Forecasting Accuracy dataset for downstream modeling and anomaly detection. We'll examine the structure, understand data sources, and visualize patterns to guide feature engineering and forecasting design.

Dataset Source: M5 Forecasting - Accuracy (Kaggle)

Use Case: Build store-item-level forecasting and anomaly detection system for Walmart’s retail demand planning.

We'll use common data science packages for analysis, visualization, and path management.

Step 10: Convert Sales Table to Long Format¶

The original sales table has one column per day (d_1 to d_1913). We melt it to long format to prepare for merging and time-series forecasting.

In [132]:
sales_long = pd.melt(
    sales,
    id_vars=['id', 'item_id', 'dept_id', 'cat_id', 'store_id', 'state_id'],
    var_name='d',
    value_name='sales'
)
sales_long = sales_long.merge(calendar[['d', 'date']], how='left', on='d')
sales_long['date'] = pd.to_datetime(sales_long['date'])
